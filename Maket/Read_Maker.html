Джин Ву открыл глаза. Потолок был незнакомым. Белый, стерильный свет резал глаза. "Где я?" — подумал он.

Suddenly, a blue screen appeared in front of his eyes. It floated in the air like a hologram.

[System: Daily Quest 'Strength Training' has arrived.]

Jin-Woo blinked several times. Was he hallucinating? But no matter how many times he rubbed his eyes, the message remained.

He tried to sit up, but his body felt heavy as lead. Pain shot through his limbs, reminding him of the dungeon.

The memories flooded back. The double dungeon. The smiling statue. The altar. And the sacrifice.

"Am I alive?" he whispered, checking his chest. His heart was beating steadily.
